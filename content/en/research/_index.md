## 电化学

在“双碳”战略的指引下，利用可再生电力将CO、CO2等废气转化为高附加值化学品，已成为实现碳资源循环利用的关键途径。本课题组致力于研究二氧化碳及其他小分子的催化转化，利用可再生能源生产甲酸、乙烯等高价值化学品。我们的目标是通过新型电解系统为可再生电合成高附加值大宗化工原料提供一条可持续的路径。


Under the guidance of the "double carbon" strategy, the use of renewable electricity to convert CO, CO2 and other waste gases into high value-added chemicals has become a key way to realize the recycling of carbon resources. We are committed to the research on catalytic conversion of CO2 and other small molecules, using renewable energy to produce high-value chemicals including formic acid, ethylene and more. We aim to provide a sustainable route for the renewable electrified synthesis of high commodity chemical feedstocks using new electrolysis system.

## AI


本课题组积极推动人工智能与催化化学的深度融合，致力于开发一种结合机器学习、理论计算和实验验证的新型研究范式，旨在开发出工业规模的实用设备。通过构建催化剂材料数据库并训练适用于分子尺度及工程规模的机器学习模型，我们可以快速预测实际应用中的关键催化参数并合理设计高性能催化剂，为高效、定向的转化过程提供智能解决方案。我们将催化、工程与人工智能相结合，共同开启电化学的新篇章。

We actively promote the deep integration of artificial intelligence and catalytic chemistry, and are committed to developing a new research paradigm combining machine learning, theoretical calculation and experimental verification, with the aim at developing practical devices at the industrial scale. By building the catalyst material database and training the machine learning model not only fit for the molecular level but as well as the microscale at engineering level, we can quickly predict the key catalytic parameters——that are truly important in the practical applications, and rationally design high-performance catalysts, providing intelligent solutions for the efficient and directional conversion process. We bring the catalysis and engineering with the AI into the new episode of the electrochemistry.
